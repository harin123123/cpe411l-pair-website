Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SoleStyle shoes.

1. What sizes do you offer?

We offer sizes ranging from US 5 to US 12 for men and US 5 to US 11 for women. Please check our size chart before ordering.

2. What is your return policy?

You can return unworn shoes within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Shoes must be in their original packaging.

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for local orders and 10-15 days for international orders.

4. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Free shipping is available for orders over $50 within the Philippines.
